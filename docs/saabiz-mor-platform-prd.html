SAABIZ

Merchant of Record (MoR) & Software Monetization Platform

Comprehensive Product Requirements Document (Final Version)

1. Product Overview

SAABIZ is a modular Merchant of Record (MoR) and monetization platform built for software developers, SaaS founders, WordPress plugin/theme creators, and digital product makers.

SABIZ acts as the legal reseller of record, managing payments, tax compliance, subscription billing, licensing, affiliate systems, and distribution infrastructure — allowing creators to focus solely on product development.

2. Target Users

  • Sellers (Developers & SaaS Founders)
  • Buyers (End-Users)
  • Affiliates (Commission-based promoters)

3. Strategic Priorities

  • Local-first payment dominance (Nigeria & Africa)
  • Primary Gateways: Paystack & Flutterwave
  • Flutterwave PayPal routing support
  • Stripe for global processing & fallback
  • Strict modular microservices architecture
  • Docker-first low-cost deployment
  • 99.99% uptime Licensing API
